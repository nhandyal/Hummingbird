How to
The Ribbon
While logged into Coursespot you will always see the Coursespot Ribbon at the top of the screen.

On the left side of the ribbon you will see the Coursespot icon. Clicking on this icon brings down the built in course browser. To hide the course browser, click on the Coursespot icon again or the small red x in the course browser.

On the right side is your name next to a little arrow. Clicking your name brings you back to your home page. Clicking on the little arrow toggles the options menu.
Adding Classes
Adding classes to Hummingbird is a breeze. You have two options to add classes. The first is to use the inline course browser, and the second is to use the Google Chrome extension. On the left is how the inline course browser looks and on the right is how the extension looks in your browser.

When using the built in course browser, the department and course sections have a built in live search feature to filter out results as you type. Once the available sections field is populated, you will notice that certain classes are greyed out while others are not. A greyed out section indicates that there is currently space available in that section. You can only add classes that are full to your Hummingbird list. To add the class to your list, just click on the section you want to add.

If you are using the Hummingbird Chrome Extension, simply navigate over to USC's onine schedule of classes. Once you locate the class you want to add to Hummingbird, highlight the section number, right click, and select "Monitor with Hummingbird".
